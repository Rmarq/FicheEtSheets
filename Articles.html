How to Get There

Toulouse is a big city where getting around or finding parking isn't always easy.
For parking, we recommend one of these two options:
  • Capitole Parking: It is possible to park in the underground parking lot at the Capitole.
    However, you will need a Crit'Air 1, 2, or 3 sticker on your car.
  • Borderouge: The Borderouge parking "relais" allows for easy parking in the north of Toulouse (no Crit'Air sticker needed) and is right next to the Borderouge Metro station.
    From Borderouge, just take the Metro to Jean Jaurès and walk to the Capitole.

Reception at "Domaine de Carles"

Meeting time

Meet at the Domaine around 7:00 PM.

Meeting Point

The Domaine de Carles is located at:
1814 Rte de Villebrumier, 31340 Villemur-sur-Tarn

Additional Information

The Domaine consists of several areas that you will discover along its main path:
  • Residential Area: On the left, after a barn, is the residential area where the owners of the Domaine live. The rooms and dormitories reserved for certain privileged guests are located upstairs.
  • Parking: On the right, there is a large parking lot where you can comfortably park your favorite vehicle.
  • Reception Hall: On the left, following the residential area, is the reception hall where we will party all night long!
  • The Park: At the end, after the hall, there is a wooded park along the Tarn. Be careful not to fall into the river if you decide to explore the park!

Informations diverses

Le domaine se compose de plusieurs espaces que vous découvrirez le long de son chemin principal :
  • Partie habitation : Sur la gauche après un hangar se trouve la partie habitation où vivent les propriétaires du domaine. Les chambres et dortoirs réservés par certains privilégiés se trouvent à l'étage.
  • Parking : Sur la droite se trouve un grand parking où vous pourrez confortablement installer votre véhicule préféré.
  • Salle de réception : Sur la gauche, à la suite des habitations, se trouve la salle de réception où l'on fera la fête toute la nuit !
  • Le parc : Au fond, après la salle, se trouve un parc boisé longeant le Tarn. Faites attention à ne pas tomber dans la rivière si vous décidez de visiter le parc !

Where to sleep

Here are some options for accommodation (in addition to staying up all night):
